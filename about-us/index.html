Our Mission

Help Fraser Valley residents move freely, recover confidently, and live well—with care that feels personal, collaborative, and close to home.

Humble Oak Wellness was conceived as the allied health arm of Humble Oak Holdings, extending the Humble Oak commitment to practical, community-rooted service into the wellness space. Our Abbotsford clinic is designed to feel welcoming and unhurried—reflecting the valley’s agricultural heritage, mountain vistas, and neighbourly spirit.

We believe the best outcomes happen when practitioners work together. Rather than sending you across town for each specialty, our integrated model keeps your care coordinated under one roof—with shared notes, aligned treatment goals, and a team that knows your story.

Fraser Valley Roots

From farm families and warehouse teams to weekend hikers on Sumas Mountain, we serve the people who keep the Fraser Valley running. Our branding draws from the oak—a symbol of strength, longevity, and deep roots—and the autumn palette of the valley’s changing seasons.

Our Values

Integrated, Not Fragmented

Your chiropractor, physiotherapist, and counsellor shouldn’t work in silos. We coordinate care so every visit builds on the last—saving you time and accelerating recovery.

Rooted in Abbotsford

We’re proud to serve Abbotsford and the wider Fraser Valley. Local ownership means local accountability—and practitioners who understand the community they treat.

Humble, Hardworking Care

The Humble Oak name reflects how we show up: no pretension, no upselling—just skilled practitioners, clear communication, and treatment plans that respect your goals and your schedule.

Part of the Humble Oak Family

Humble Oak Wellness operates under Humble Oak Holdings, alongside ventures like Humble Oak Solutions (operations and IT consulting). While each entity serves a different need, they share a common philosophy: practical expertise, community focus, and a willingness to do the hard work that makes a real difference.
