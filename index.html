Welcome to Humble Oak Wellness

Humble Oak Wellness is an allied health clinic rooted in Abbotsford and proudly serving the Fraser Valley. As a subsidiary of Humble Oak Holdings, we bring the same grounded, community-first values that define the Humble Oak family—applied to whole-person health and recovery.

Our clinic brings chiropractic, physiotherapy, massage therapy, acupuncture, counselling, and active rehabilitation under one roof. Practitioners collaborate on your care plan so you spend less time navigating referrals and more time feeling better.
